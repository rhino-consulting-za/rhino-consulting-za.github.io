About Us

Rhino Consulting is a South African aviation consultancy specialising in the full spectrum of aviation operations. With deep expertise in South African Civil Aviation Regulations (SA CARs 2011), we support operators, training organisations, and individuals across all phases of aviation.

Our team brings hands-on experience in flight training, charter operations, airworthiness, and regulatory compliance — helping you navigate the complex world of SA aviation with confidence.
